Sunday Services

Our Celebration Service is at 9:00 a.m.

The celebration service is an upbeat, engaging time for all ages. The Bethany Praise Band with electric guitar and bass, keyboard and drums brings a fresh approach to worship. This service has a much more expressive feel to it. Those who are new to church or enjoy lively and engaging worship feel at home in this service.
